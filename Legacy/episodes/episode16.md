## Secrets and Clues

- The Beast has been stripping beast men of their identity and transforming them into normal beasts. 

- The Beastmen are split into multiple tribes vying for control of the same resources, they each worship a totem animal that relates to their tribe. 
    - Bear
    - Wolf
    - Rat
    - Boar 

- Some members of the tribes see the lack of resources as a problem and want to mend ties with the "No Furs" to trade resources they have gathered; others want to unite the tribes to go to war with the men.

- The Red Hand has been working with the Rat Men in order to get knowledge of the woods and the whereabouts of the "Rod Piece"; they are responsible for releasing "The Beast".

- The woods are far old and untamed; some creatures long thought to be gone still live there. Spirits are also common in that area.
